    X-WAVE™️ - CELLULITE & CONTOUR THERAPY

    ENHANCE THE TEXTURE OF YOUR SKIN

    X-WAVE™️ is a shockwave therapy clinically proven to treat cellulite and contour the body. This treatment works by breaking down fat under the skin and stimulating lymphatic drainage to remove excess toxins from the body. It also aids in collagen production for the skin to appear smoother, healthier, and more youthful.

    WHAT ARE THE TREATMENT ADVANTAGES?

  • Breaks down fatty deposits and cellulite
  • Improves muscle tone
  • Smooths and rejuvenates skin
  • Tightens skin and underlying connective tissues
  • Reduces circumference of treated area
  • Increases the firmness of skin

    • Recommended

  • 4-6 treatments over a course of few weeks
  • Visible results after a first session
  • A great treatment when combined with ULTRA Body Tightening

    ULTRA BODY TIGHTENING

    LOOK AMAZING FROM EVERY ANGLE

    Combining radio frequency and ultrasound to precisely tighten skin and address body concerns from head to toe; works on even the most wrinkled and problematic areas.

    This treatment uses controlled heating and cooling to ensure energy is delivered to various depths in the tissue without discomfort. Changing power and cooling during treatment process allows the provider to treat deep tissue all the way up to the superficial layer.

    Recommended

  • 2-4 treatments to be scheduled 7-10 days apart
  • Treatment takes between 15-30 minutes per specific body part
  • Best results are achieved when treatment is combined with exercise.
